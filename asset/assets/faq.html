• What is Yahoo Gemini?

Yahoo Gemini is the only marketplace that offers search and native advertising in one advertising platform. With Gemini, you are empowered with insightful data, brand-safe premium content and advanced technologies to deliver engaging search and native advertising campaigns that really drive results.

  • What are product ads?

Product Ads or Dynamic Product Ads (DPA) are feed-based retargeting ads that are personalized to each user’s unique shopping history, helping you re-engage your site visitors with those missed opportunities.

  • Where will the products show?

    Yahoo! Home page, Yahoo! Finance, Yahoo! Sports, Huffpost and other Yahoo! And AOL sites.

  • How do I change product image, title and description?

The product names and descriptions are managed within your Shopify store. You will be able to change the product information by going into the ‘Product’ section.

  • How do I show the selected products instead of all of them?

    All available products in the Shopify store will be selected for the Gemini product ad campaigns. To remove the product from the Gemini campaign, the user has to remove it from the Shopify product listing.
  • How do I setup the campaign schedule?

After you start the campaign, the campaign will be active unless you manually pause it. For the advanced users who would like to setup a schedule, one can go to Gemini directly to manage the campaigns. Gemini can be accessed at https://gemini.yahoo.com/ 

  • Campaign and Bid per click best practices
  • Daily budget range $50-$500 to start the new campaign depending on the overall marketing budget; the budget will be paced evenly over the day with the adjustment based on traffic pattern.
  • Recommended bid per click is between $0.30-$1 in U.S. depending on the audience targeting strategy. The campaigns set through Gemini will have a default bid which is an average across all different user funnel targeting strategies.
  • How do I access the report?

As soon as the time window is selected, you will be able to see the report within the same UI if there is data. The available metrics are budget, clicks and Cost per Click. The available date ranges are: Today, Yesterday, This week (Mon - Today), Last week (Mon - Sun), Last 7 days, Last 14 days, This month, Last month, Last 30 days.

  • Can I export the report to other formats?

This feature is currently not supported.

  • Terms

Budget - The maximum amount you are willing to pay a day for the campaign.

Bid per click - The maximum amount you are willing to pay if someone clicks on your ad.
