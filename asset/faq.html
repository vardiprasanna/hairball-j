Terms

  • What is Yahoo Gemini?

Yahoo Gemini is the only marketplace that offers search and native advertising in one advertising platform. With Gemini, you are empowered with insightful data, brand-safe premium content and advanced technologies to deliver engaging search and native advertising campaigns that really drive results.

  • What are product ads?

Product Ads or Dynamic Product Ads (DPA) are feed-based retargeting ads that are personalized to each user’s unique shopping history, helping you re-engage your site visitors with those missed opportunities.

  • Where will the products show?

    Yahoo! Home page, Yahoo! Finance, Yahoo! Sports, Huffpost and other Yahoo! And AOL sites.

  • How do I get started?

    To get started, you will need a Yahoo! account and a Gemini account. Please consider the following scenarios:
  1. I have neither the Yahoo! account nor a Gemini account.
  1. Please sign up a Yahoo! Account by clicking on ‘Register’ button on Gemini App in Shopify or going to https://gemini.yahoo.com directly.

  1. Click on ‘Sign up’ button on top and fill out the account information and verify the phone number.

  1. Create a Gemini account.

  1. Setup the credit card information by going to the wrench icon on top and select ‘Billing’.


  1. When the Gemini account setup is completed, please launch the Gemini app on Shopify again and press ‘Sign In’.

  1. I have a Yahoo! account but not a Gemini account.
  1. Click on ‘Register’ button on the Gemini App in Shopify or go to https://gemini.yahoo.com directly.

  1. Click on ‘Sign In’ on top to login into the Yahoo! account.

  1. Click on ‘Create Gemini account’ on top and fill in the required information. 

  1. Setup the credit card information by going to the wrench icon on top and select ‘Billing’. 


  1. After the Gemini account setup is completed, please launch the Gemini app on Shopify again and click on ‘Sign In’.

  • How do I change product image, title and description?

The product names and descriptions are managed within your Shopify store. You will be able to change the product information by going into the ‘Product’ section.

  • How do I select the products for the product ad campaign?

    By default, all products are selected; however the products can be setup through the Gemini UI at https://gemini.yahoo.com/. 
  • How do I setup the campaign schedule?

After you start the campaign, the campaign will be active unless you manually pause it. The schedule can be setup through the Gemini UI at https://gemini.yahoo.com/. 

  • Campaign and Bid per click best practices
  1. Daily budget range $50-$500 to start the new campaign depending on the overall marketing budget; the budget will be paced evenly over the day with the adjustment based on traffic pattern.
  2. Recommended bid per click is between $0.30-$1 in U.S. depending on the audience targeting strategy. The campaigns set through Gemini will have a default bid which is an average across all different user funnel targeting strategies.
  • How do I access the report?

The report can be accessed directly within the same UI if there is data. The available metrics are budget, clicks and Cost per Click. The available date ranges are: Today, Yesterday, This week (Mon - Today), Last week (Mon - Sun), Last 7 days, Last 14 days, This month, Last month, Last 30 days. More analytics data can be accessed through Gemini UI.

  • How do I send the app feedback?

Please reach us at helloGemini@cc.yahoo-inc.com for any feedback or inquiries.

  • Terms

Budget - The maximum amount you are willing to pay a day for the campaign.

Bid per click - The maximum amount you are willing to pay if someone clicks on your ad.
